# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Couples, families, companies, and event organizers in Madrid, Guadalajara, and nearby municipalities who are comparing photo booth, video booth, mirror booth, 360 platform, and event extras for weddings and celebrations. They usually arrive with a date, venue, guest count, and a need for a clear, trustworthy quote.

## Product Purpose

Pimpim Fotomatones is a public marketing site for turning visitors into qualified budget requests through clear services, local pages, real inclusions, WhatsApp contact, reviews, and event proof. Success means visitors understand what Pimpim offers, trust the team, and ask for a budget with enough context to answer quickly.

## Brand Personality

Warm, playful, and reliable. The site should feel like "Lo fácil nunca fue lo divertido": energetic and celebratory, but still professional enough for weddings and company events.

## Anti-references

Do not make the site look like a generic AI landing page, a cold SaaS page, or a vague stock-event template. Avoid abstract decoration replacing real event imagery. Avoid hiding the offer behind overly broad marketing copy or price-table-heavy pages.

## Design Principles

- Show the experience before explaining it.
- Keep the path to WhatsApp and budget requests obvious.
- Separate services, inclusions, and extras so clients do not confuse what is included with what is optional.
- Use real Pimpim visuals whenever trust matters.
- Keep the static site fast, responsive, and easy to publish.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast and readable typography on mobile. Respect reduced motion. Keep CTAs keyboard-accessible, labels clear, and third-party widgets isolated enough that the page remains usable if they fail to load.
